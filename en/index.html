Information

For whom is this service

For patients who require psychiatric care, but whose routines demand total privacy protection, schedule flexibility, and the elimination of commuting across the capital of São Paulo.

How is the care conducted?

Upon confirmation of scheduling and payment, care is provided at your preferred location — residence, office, or hotel. The consultation features the same depth and technical rigor as a medical office, including a comprehensive clinical evaluation, diagnostic definition, therapeutic planning, and prescription when indicated. All consultations can be carried out at your preferred location. As this is a private, highly individualized medical service with time exclusively reserved for the patient, all consultations are scheduled and paid in advance. There are no complimentary follow-up visits.

Where is the service provided?

Residences, corporations, offices, and hotels located exclusively within the city of São Paulo.

Who provides the treatment?

All appointments are conducted personally and directly by Dr. Elquer Rodrigues.

How do I schedule an appointment?

The initial request is made via WhatsApp. Following a brief analysis of clinical and geographical availability, the appointment time is confirmed directly with the physician.

Why the name ANGMER?

The name ANGMER was inspired by Angela Merkel—not for politics, but for her quiet and effective leadership. She governed with serenity, consistency, and solid decision-making. Her credibility came from the quality of her actions, not from grand speeches. This stance inspires the essence of ANGMER. Discretion, competence, and consistency are my core pillars.
